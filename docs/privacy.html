Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

Deal Bazaar India ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

We collect minimal data to improve your experience:

Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies. Your wishlist is stored in your browser's localStorage only — it never leaves your device.
